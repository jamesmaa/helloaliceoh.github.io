Quad
Company Overview
CampusCred Inc. d/b/a Quad was co-founded in June 2010 by Sagar Shah et al., and was accepted into Y Combinator Summer 2011. The team then went through some transitions and brought on Mark Miyashita, formerly Leada, Asana, and Yelp, as co-founder to relaunch as a new product, Quad, a task management app for college students. They are based in San Francisco and plan to launch in January 2016.
Product Overview
Quad is an automated, personalized to-do list for to answer the question: "What do I need to get done today?" At the start of each school term, students can indicate their classes and the college they are enrolled in, and Quad will generate a calendar of assignments, projects, and exam personalized for each student's class schedule. Then, students can ask questions, discuss, and receive notifications about upcoming deadlines.

Quad's mission is to answer the most pressing questions students face while navigating from college to career and beyond.
